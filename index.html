Happy Birthday, my love. 🤍

I don't really know where to begin because there are so many things I want to tell you. Today is the birthday of someone who means so much to me. Thank you for being part of my life, because without you, I don't think my days would feel this warm and beautiful.

On this new chapter of your life, I only wish the very best for you. May God always guide and protect every step you take, bless you with good health, endless happiness, abundant blessings, and grant every dream you're working so hard to achieve.

I hope your heart is always filled with peace, your shoulders are always strong enough to carry every burden, and whatever challenges you're facing will slowly find their way to a happy ending.

Thank you for coming into my life. Thank you for becoming my safe place, the person I can always talk to, laugh with, cry to, and simply be myself around.

Thank you for always trying to understand me, even when I'm being childish, stubborn, jealous, moody, or when I accidentally make things difficult for you. I know I'm far from perfect, but thank you for choosing to stay.

If one day the world feels too heavy for you, please don't forget that you don't have to carry everything alone. I may not be able to solve every problem for you, but I promise I'll always be here to stand beside you.

So whenever you're tired, come back to me. Whenever you're sad, tell me about it. Whenever life feels overwhelming, hold my hand—we'll get through it together.

I also have one little wish for us. As we grow older, I hope we continue to become better people for each other. If we ever have misunderstandings, let's talk them through. If we ever face problems, let's find the solution together. Please don't ever give up on us just because of one bad day.

Do you know what I'm most grateful for? It's meeting you. Your presence has turned so many ordinary days into something special.

Even the smallest moments—talking to you, laughing with you, or simply knowing that you're doing okay—are enough to make my heart feel at peace.

I hope this new age brings you more smiles than tears, more happiness than disappointments, and countless beautiful moments. May God continue to watch over you wherever life takes you.

Lastly, thank you for being the wonderful "Abang" that I love so dearly.

Happy Birthday once again, my favorite person.

I love you today, tomorrow, and for as long as God allows me to.

With all my love,

— Your Adek, Raisa 🤍
